THE POOR FOUR

The Four Traps That Keep Service Businesses Stuck

Most service business owners fall into one of these four patterns. Each one costs you time, money, and growth. Sound familiar?

TRAP 01

The DIY Marketer

You're running ads between jobs, posting on social at midnight, and your "marketing strategy" lives in your head. You're working harder than ever — and leads are still inconsistent.

TRAP 02

The Agency Hopper

You've hired two or three agencies. Each one promised the moon. None delivered. Now you're skeptical of anyone who says they can help — but you still need help.

TRAP 03

The Feast-or-Famine Owner

Business comes in waves. One month you're slammed, the next you're wondering where everyone went. You can't plan, can't hire, and can't grow because you never know what's coming.

TRAP 04

The Referral-Only Business

Word-of-mouth got you this far, but it won't get you where you want to go. You have zero control over when the next job shows up — and that ceiling is getting lower every year.

The common thread? You're spending energy on marketing instead of running your business. There's a better way.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the things people ask most.

What does "month-to-month, no long-term lock-in" actually mean?

+

It means exactly what it says. There's no 6-month or 12-month contract. You pay monthly. If you want to stop, you give us 30 days notice. You keep everything we built — ad accounts, landing pages, automations, data. It's yours.

How much does it cost?

+

Pricing depends on your market, your goals, and the scope of the engine we build. We'll give you a clear number on the discovery call — no hidden fees, no surprises. We don't publish pricing because every business is different and we'd rather give you an honest quote than a misleading range.

Do I need to provide content or creatives?

+

No. We handle everything — ad copy, images, landing page content, email sequences. If you have existing photos or brand assets, great, we'll use them. If not, we create everything from scratch using AI-powered tools and our creative team.

What platforms do you advertise on?

+

Primarily Google Ads and Meta (Facebook/Instagram). Depending on your industry and market, we may also recommend YouTube, TikTok, or local directories. We go where your customers are — not where the trendiest platform is.

What makes this different from a regular marketing agency?

+

A regular agency sells you hours and tasks. We build a system that compounds. Every month your engine runs, it gets smarter — better targeting, better creatives, better conversion rates. Traditional agencies hit a ceiling because human effort doesn't scale. AI-powered engines do.
