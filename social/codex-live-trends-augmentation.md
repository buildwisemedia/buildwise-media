# BWM Social Augmentation Memo — Current 2026

## LinkedIn

1. **What Is Winning Right Now**

   The best B2B posts are moving away from company-page polish and toward expert-led proof: native document carousels, short diagnostic videos, and “here is what we found” operating notes. LinkedIn’s own 2026 B2B guidance points to people-powered thought leadership and video as trust builders, with creator and employee voices carrying more weight than brand claims ([B2B creators](https://www.linkedin.com/business/marketing/blog/trends-tips/b2b-marketing-insights-creators-thought-leadership), [B2B video](https://www.linkedin.com/business/marketing/blog/trends-tips/b2b-insights-video-controls-conversation)).

   Generic plans will say “post thought leadership.” The missing mechanic is **diagnostic utility**: posts that help an owner see a hidden business leak in under 20 seconds, then save or send it to a partner.

2. **Contrarian / Under-used Angle**

   Make BWM feel like the calm second brain behind the owner, not another loud growth shop. Use “system diagnosis” posts: one messy owner problem, one leak map, one verified next step.

3. **Creative Direction**

   Native PDF carousel: “The Leak You Feel But Can’t See.” First slide is a lo-fi overhead iPhone shot of a dark desk, a paper intake form, a ringing phone, and a half-finished estimate. Cool daylight, grain, hand-held framing. Add a thin Triangulation overlay: three #F0FF00 vertices labeled “call,” “handoff,” “follow-up,” connected by hairlines on #050507. No vendor UI. No founder face. Inter headline, JetBrains Mono micro-labels, one yellow verdict-dot on the break.

4. **2-Line Sample Caption**

   > We map the handoff leak before we build the system to fix it.  
   > Get My Free Revenue Leak Map: /go/revenue-leak-map

## Facebook

1. **What Is Winning Right Now**

   Facebook is rewarding original, human content in Feed and Reels, while reducing reach for copycat or low-effort reposts. Meta says views and watch time for original Reels roughly doubled in the second half of 2025 versus the same period in 2024 ([Meta original content update](https://about.fb.com/news/2026/03/rewarding-original-creators-on-facebook/)).

   For service-business owners, the winning mix is not broad “brand awareness.” It is **local trust plus comment-worthy relief**: Reels that show a familiar owner problem, posts that invite a real business detail, and fast replies that feel useful instead of salesy.

2. **Contrarian / Under-used Angle**

   Use Facebook as the “owner relief” channel. Less future-of-AI, more “this is why your day keeps leaking money even when your team is busy.”

3. **Creative Direction**

   Vertical Reel, 20-30 seconds. Scene: service owner sitting in a work truck or back office after a long day, well-lit face, phone on the dash or desk, missed calls and notes visible but not readable. The camera is close, candid, slightly imperfect. A #F0FF00 Triangulation path animates from “missed call” to “late reply” to “lost job.” Final frame: dark #050507 card with a single verdict-dot and “MAP THE LEAK” in Inter.

4. **2-Line Sample Caption**

   > We find the small breaks that make good leads vanish when the day gets busy.  
   > Get My Free Revenue Leak Map: /go/revenue-leak-map

## Instagram

1. **What Is Winning Right Now**

   Instagram is pushing original content harder across Reels, photos, and carousels, with unoriginal repost accounts losing recommendation reach. Its 2026 direction favors posts that feel native, human, and worth sending in DMs ([Instagram original-content shift](https://www.digitalcameraworld.com/tech/social-media/two-years-later-instagram-is-finally-giving-photographers-the-same-protection-as-videographers-with-this-key-change)). Broader 2026 trend reports also point to less-polished, real-world storytelling and serialized content as a way to stand out in an AI-heavy feed ([Sprout 2026 trends](https://sproutsocial.com/insights/social-media-trends/)).

   Generic plans will over-index on trends and audio. The better mechanic is **sendable clarity**: one visual that makes an owner say, “This is exactly what is happening here.”

2. **Contrarian / Under-used Angle**

   Make “unsexy operations” visually desirable. Show the quiet leak: the sticky note, the call log, the quote draft, the late-night laptop. Turn business friction into a sharp visual system.

3. **Creative Direction**

   R018e photo carousel or Reel. Bright daylight, cool ambient light, candid iPhone look, visible grain. Frame a real-feeling back office: coffee, job folders, whiteboard, phone, invoice stack. No polished studio. Overlay a minimal Triangulation leak path in #F0FF00 with three labels: “asked,” “waited,” “left.” Final slide is a dark #050507 “verified leak map” card with a single yellow CTA-pull mark.

4. **2-Line Sample Caption**

   > We turn messy follow-up into a clear path you can trust.  
   > Get My Free Revenue Leak Map: /go/revenue-leak-map

## Twitter/X

1. **What Is Winning Right Now**

   X still rewards sharp, real-time point of view, but it is no longer just a text feed. Vertical video is now X’s fastest-growing surface, with X reporting 100M+ daily vertical-video viewers and about 20% of daily time spent in that format ([X vertical video](https://business.x.com/en/products/vertical-video-ads)). In parallel, 2026 social strategy is shifting toward fast response, social search, and brand intelligence rather than fixed content calendars ([Hootsuite 2026 trends](https://www.hootsuite.com/research/social-trends)).

   Generic plans will say “post threads.” The missed mechanic is **receipt-led compression**: one claim, one proof point, one diagram, one next step.

2. **Contrarian / Under-used Angle**

   Do not chase hot takes. Own the “receipts, not guesses” lane. X can be BWM’s public proof wire: short teardown threads, redacted leak maps, and blunt operator truths.

3. **Creative Direction**

   Single image plus short thread. Visual is a dark #050507 forensic card: three redacted timestamps, one blurred service-business workflow, and a #F0FF00 Triangulation line showing where the leak starts. Keep it sparse enough to read on mobile. Optional 8-second vertical cutdown: hand draws the three-point leak map on paper, then hard-cuts to the finished dark card.

4. **2-Line Sample Caption**

   > We use receipts, not guesses: one missed step can cost the whole job.  
   > Get My Free Revenue Leak Map: /go/revenue-leak-map

## Creative System Notes

- Use one repeatable visual grammar: dark #050507 canvas, #F0FF00 as the action signal, Inter for clear claims, JetBrains Mono for proof labels, and the Triangulation mark as a working diagnostic layer, not decoration.
- Keep R018e photography consistent: iPhone framing, hand-held, cool light, grain, real rooms, bright faces, and candid business moments. Avoid glossy SaaS mockups, stock smiles, and abstract AI art.
- Every post should show a leak, a proof cue, or a fix path. No decorative-only atoms.
- Do not make BWM look like a lead-gen shop. The headline idea is always: verifiable custom AI systems. Leads, bookings, and follow-up wins are receipts.
- Build a recurring series across all four channels: “Leak Map of the Week.” Same system, different native format: LinkedIn carousel, Facebook Reel, Instagram carousel/Reel, X receipt thread.
- Keep public copy plain: one owner pain, one system promise, one CTA. Use “we,” “the team,” or “our system.” Never use founder-face framing, vendor names, naked pricing, or revenue qualifiers.
