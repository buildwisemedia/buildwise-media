SMS Account Notification Opt-In

Updated: April 26, 2026

This page is the opt-in capture point for Buildwise Media LLC's Account Notification SMS program. It is intended for authorized Buildwise Media account holders — the business owner, principal, and any individual the principal explicitly authorizes to receive operational alerts on behalf of the company. It is not a consumer marketing list and is not used for promotional outreach.

By submitting the form below with your phone number and the explicit consent checkbox checked, you grant Buildwise Media LLC permission to send you SMS messages relating to the operation of your Buildwise Media account.

What you will receive

Messages sent under this program are limited to the operational categories below. We do not send marketing, promotional, or third-party content under this program.

  • Lead alerts — when a prospective customer submits a form on a Buildwise Media-operated website (yours or another Buildwise Media client's), with the lead's name, contact, and source.
  • Incident alerts — when a Buildwise Media internal system or worker enters a failed or degraded state that affects your account.
  • Task alerts — when an automated audit assigns an action to you that requires owner attention.
  • Account updates — help, support, and confirmation messages tied to this opt-in itself.

Representative samples of the actual content sent under this program:

  • BWM lead alert — design2sell: Lead Name / lead@example.com / 404-555-0100. source=contact-form. Reply STOP to opt out.
  • BWM incident — worker bwm-ads-reporter status changed to failed at 2026-04-26T09:15Z. Reply STOP to opt out.
  • BWM task — Connector audit P0: red cell townsend-realty-group cell 3 Meta Pixel missing. Reply STOP to opt out.
  • Buildwise Media account notifications. For support email hello@buildwisemedia.com. Msg freq varies, typically 5–30/day. Msg&data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help.

Frequency and cost

Message frequency varies based on the volume of leads and operational events on your account. Typical volume is 5–30 messages per day. Message and data rates may apply per your wireless carrier. Buildwise Media does not charge a fee for receiving these messages.

How to stop receiving messages

You can stop receiving Account Notification SMS at any time by replying with any of the following keywords to a message from Buildwise Media: STOP, STOPALL, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, QUIT, OPTOUT, or REVOKE. You will receive one confirmation message and will not receive further Account Notification messages from us. Reply HELP at any time to receive support contact information.

You may also email hello@buildwisemedia.com to be removed from this program.

How we use your phone number

The phone number you submit on this page is used solely to send the Account Notification messages described above. It is stored in our internal Buildwise Media system and in our messaging infrastructure provider's account configuration. It is not shared with third parties for marketing purposes, sold, or used for any other promotional outreach. See our full Privacy Policy for our broader data-handling practices.

Each successful submission of this form creates an auditable consent record — including timestamp, the phone number you provided, your IP address, and your browser user-agent — retained by Buildwise Media for the duration of your enrollment in the program plus three years thereafter, in accordance with carrier and TCR record-keeping guidance for A2P 10DLC programs.
