Self-hosted brand webfonts — Buildwise Media (buildwisemedia.com)
================================================================

These fonts are the LOCKED Triangulation brand typefaces (Inter display/body +
JetBrains Mono micro). They are self-hosted (no third-party CDN) per the BWM
no-vendor-fetch posture. Both are licensed under the SIL Open Font License 1.1,
which permits self-hosting and webfont embedding provided this license notice
accompanies the font files.

----------------------------------------------------------------

  Source:   https://github.com/rsms/inter  (rsms.me/inter)  Inter v4.x
  Upstream: https://rsms.me/inter/font-files/InterVariable.woff2
  License:  SIL Open Font License 1.1  (https://openfontlicense.org)
            Copyright 2016 The Inter Project Authors
            (https://github.com/rsms/inter)
  Subset:   Reduced to a generous Western + symbol/arrow/math/box-drawing/

            punctuation, currency, letterlike, arrows, math operators,
            box drawing, geometric shapes, misc symbols, dingbats).
            Greek / Cyrillic / Vietnamese-only letterforms removed (site is

Regenerating these files
------------------------
The build pipeline that produced these subsets (fonttools Subsetter, woff2
flavor) is documented in implementation-notes.md. To update a font, bump the
-vN suffix (rename here, in src/styles/global.css @font-face src, and in the
BaseLayout.astro preload links) so the immutable /fonts/* cache (public/_headers)
serves the new bytes.
